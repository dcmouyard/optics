v = λf
  • v = velocity (m/s)
  • λ = wavelength (m)
  • f = frequency (Hz)
c = λf
  • c = speed of light in a vacuum (2.99792458 x 10⁸ m/s)
  • λ = wavelength of light (m)
  • f = frequency of light (Hz)
c = nv
  • n = index of refraction for a medium
  • v = speed of light in the medium (m/s)
  • c = speed of light in a vacuum (2.99792458 x 10⁸ m/s)
sinθ₁n₁ = sinθ₂n₂
  • θ₁ = angle of incidence (°)
  • n₁ = index of refraction for first medium
  • θ₂ = angle of refraction (°)
  • n₂ = index of refraction for second medium
sinθ₁ / sinθ₂ = f₁ / f₂ = λ₁ / λ₂ = n₂ / n₁
  • θ₁ = angle of incidence (°)
  • θ₂ = angle of refraction (°)
  • f₁ = frequency of light in first medium (Hz)
  • f₂ = frequency of light in second medium (Hz)
  • λ₁ = wavelength of light in first medium (m)
  • λ₂ = wavelength of light in second medium (m)
  • n₁ = index of refraction for first medium
  • n₂ = index of refraction for second medium
P = 1 / f
  • P = optical power (1/m)
  • f = focal length (m)
E = hν
  • E = energy of a photon (J)
  • h = Planck’s constant (6.6260755 x 10-34 J·s)
  • ν = frequency of the photon (Hz)
θ = 2arctan( Df - Di / 2l )
  • θ = beam divergence (°)
  • Df = final beam diameter (m)
  • Di = initial beam diameter (m)
  • l = length between the two beam diameter measurements (m)
