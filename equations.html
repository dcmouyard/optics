Equations Expand All E = hν
  • E = energy of a photon (J)
  • h = Planck’s constant (6.6260755 x 10-34 Js)
  • ν = frequency of the photon (Hz)
n = c/v
  • n = index of refraction for a medium
  • c = speed of light in a vacuum (2.99792458 x 108 m/s)
  • v = speed of light in the medium (m/s)
sinθ1n1 = sinθ2n2
  • θ1 = angle of incidence (°)
  • n1 = index of refraction for first medium (°)
  • θ2 = angle of refraction
  • n2 = index of refraction for second medium
sinθ1 / sinθ2 = ν1 / ν2 = λ1 / λ2 = n2 / n1
  • θ1 = angle of incidence (°)
  • θ2 = angle of refraction (°)
  • ν1 = frequency of light in first medium (Hz)
  • ν2 = frequency of light in second medium (Hz)
  • λ1 = wavelength of light in first medium (m)
  • λ2 = wavelength of light in second medium (m)
  • n1 = index of refraction for first medium
  • n2 = index of refraction for second medium
λν = c
  • λ = wavelength of light (m)
  • ν = frequency of light (Hz)
  • c = speed of light in a vacuum (2.99792458 x 108 m/s)
